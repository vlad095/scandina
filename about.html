About Us

History

Our story begins in 2016 when Vuk Simić and Nikola Milosavljević, who both have an extensive national and international experience within hospitality, came up with an idea of opening a staffing agency that creates professional relationships between dedicated workers and trusted employers in the Nordic countries. After 3 years of developing our idea and getting well known with the Nordic cultures, we have established a recruitment agency in 2019, based in Belgrade, Serbia.

Our work

What makes us different from others is that we provide each client with maximum quality and security. We have the necessary experience and ability to provide our clients with the highest level of service. Scandina equally cares about our job-searching associates, as well as for our candidates who are looking for a job.

Scandina in future

Our vision is to become the leading staffing agency for hospitality in Europe. We want to meet the needs of all our clients by providing high quality work and safe business.
